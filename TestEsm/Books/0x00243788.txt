Last week we had a major spike and all power went out. It lasted for two hours. People came outside, and the Societies mingled and joked like it was a holiday.

I wanted to shout - what about the Charions? Everyone wanders around blissfully unconcerned that there are dangerous predators literally everywhere on this  planet. The only thing that keeps them away are the turrets and sound mechanisms (not sure what to call them) on the wall.

We are so utterly dependent on the Facility. The only reason I'm here is because Franklin's the only one that seems to give a damn.
