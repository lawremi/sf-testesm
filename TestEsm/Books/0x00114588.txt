New Atlantis.

Many believe that this shimmering city is a utopia. A testament to human ingenuity, and a symbol of humanity's place in the galaxy. 

But is it really? 

[What follows is a lengthy write-up exposing the dark underbelly of New Atlantis. It reads like a manifesto of sorts. Tommy argues that the ruling class should be overthrown and that journalists, such as himself, will lead us all into a new age of prosperity.]
